Boston Film Festival

Boston Film Festival (BFF), is an International, competitive festival. BFF's mission is to provide a public forum in Boston in order to advance public interest in films and the independent production of films.
To draw worldwide attention to Boston as a center for cinema. To encourage the rights of all Boston residents to access and experience the power of independent filmmaking, and to promote artistic excellence and the creative freedom of artists without censure. BFF, inc. is a not-for-profit organization.
